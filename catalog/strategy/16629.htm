CARD STRATEGY

Sacrifice abilities tend to be a lot stronger than they look at first. For instance, with a Fallen Angel you can:
• Get rid of a creature your opponent is about to steal.
• Sacrifice a creature your opponent has blocked and is about to kill anyway, in order to have the Angel deal 2 more damage.
• Kill all your creatures if someone is playing Living Death (from the TempestTM expansion).
• Sacrifice a small creature in order to save the Angel, if it's about to take 3 damage.
A big strength of the ability is that it requires no tapping and no mana, which means you can play it any time; you don't have to keep resources available.
