CARD STRATEGY

Call of the Wild works best if you somehow know what the top card of your library is. You might play a Dream Cache, for instance, and put a big creature from your hand on top of your library. You can then use Call of the Wild to put that creature into play! If you use cards from Magic expansions, you can probably do this without ever leaving green--that is, many expansions contain green cards that let you change or look at your library's top card.
