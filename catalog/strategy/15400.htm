CARD STRATEGY

Having one mana available for emergencies is a better ability than you might think. It makes it harder for your opponent to play Power Sink on your spells, for instance. Or you might use it to regenerate a larger creature. Also, there may be a time when it's crucial to play a big spell right now, and you can't wait to draw one more land.
Blood Pet can be especially good in decks with ways to bring back your creatures from the graveyard in a group, such as Living Death from the TempestTM expansion.
