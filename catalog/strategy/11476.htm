CARD STRATEGY

First strike is an excellent ability in red because it combines so well with direct damage. For instance, if you attack with your Sabretooth Tiger and your opponent blocks with a 4/4 creature, a Shock will allow you to kill your opponent's creature without losing the Tiger. Notice that if the Tiger didn't have first strike, this tactic would be less effective--you'd still kill your opponent's creature, but you'd lose the Tiger as well.
That said, a Sabretooth Tiger is a little small for the amount of mana you're paying for it, so it's probably not worth putting in your deck. But if you see a red creature with first strike that has better stats compared with its mana cost, it's likely to work quite nicely in a deck that also uses direct damage.
