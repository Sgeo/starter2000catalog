CARD STRATEGY

Like Grizzly Bears, Trained Armodon is an example of how, when it comes to brute force, green gets the most creature for its mana. In contrast, white has Regal Unicorn (a 2/3 creature for ), blue has Horned Turtle (a 1/4 creature for ), black has Python (a 3/2 creature for ), and red has Balduvian Barbarians (a 3/2 creature for ).
