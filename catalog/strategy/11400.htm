CARD STRATEGY

Merfolk of the Pearl Trident is a perfect example of the fact that, when it comes to creatures without flying, blue is the weakest color in the game. For one mana, each of the other colors gets a better creature: green gets Llanowar Elves, a 1/1 creature that can tap for mana; red gets Raging Goblin, a 1/1 creature that can attack the turn it comes under your control; white gets Tundra Wolves, a 1/1 creature with first strike; and black (the second weakest creature color) gets Bog Rats, a 1/1 creature that can't be blocked by Walls. Of course, blue has other strengths, such as counterspelling, that make up for this weakness.
That said, Merfolk of the Pearl Trident is still playable with Lord of Atlantis in a Merfolk deck, which will probably also use several other cheap Merfolk, plus counterspells.
